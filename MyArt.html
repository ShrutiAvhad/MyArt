About

Hi, I'm Shruti Avhad, and art is not just a passion—it's a part of who I am. For as long as I can remember, art has been my way of exploring the world, expressing emotions, and connecting with others. Every brushstroke, color, and shape holds a piece of my soul. From painting to sculpture, I find joy in creating pieces that tell stories, evoke emotions, and spark imagination. Art is my love, my life, and my way of seeing the beauty in everything around me. Welcome to my world of creativity—where each creation is an extension of myself.
